Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Overview

Manna ("we", "our", "the app") is a Bible study app. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

Manna stores all your data locally on your device. This includes:

  • Your name and preferences (chosen during onboarding)
  • Saved verses, journal entries, and reading progress
  • Companion name, level, and XP
  • Prayer timer statistics
  • App settings (theme, notification preferences)

This data never leaves your device and is not sent to any server.

Subscriptions

If you subscribe to Manna, payment is processed by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives an anonymous identifier to verify your subscription. We do not see or store your payment information.

Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking services. We do not collect usage data, device identifiers, or location information.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store — processes subscription payments
  • RevenueCat — manages subscription entitlements using anonymous identifiers
  • Expo — delivers app updates (no personal data shared)

Push Notifications

If you enable daily verse reminders, we use Apple's push notification service. Your notification token is stored locally on your device. We do not operate a notification server.

Children's Privacy

Manna is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app. No data remains on any server after deletion.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, email us at support@getmannabible.app.
